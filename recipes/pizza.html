Pizza

Description:

Wood fired or brick oven pizza is exactly what its names imply. It's a type of pizza that's cooked in a brick oven. Instead of being heated by electricity or gas, a brick pizza oven employs burning wood to cook pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olive Oil

Step:

  1. First you are going to want to make the dough
  2. You then want to knead it and put the tomato sauce on top
  3. After you will add grated Mozzarella
  4. Add Basil and Pepperoni
  5. Put olive oil on the pizza and put it into the oven
